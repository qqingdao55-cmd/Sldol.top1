1. Job Responsibilities

Party B agrees to perform the duties assigned by Party A, including but not limited to:

  1. Promoting the company’s products, services, or brand through approved channels.
  2. Inviting and attracting new users, clients, or partners to join or engage with the company.
  3. Participating in marketing campaigns, outreach activities, and communication tasks arranged by Party A.
  4. Providing timely updates, reports, and feedback regarding promotional progress.
  5. Following all instructions, guidelines, and policies provided by Party A regarding promotional work.

Party B shall complete tasks with professionalism, diligence, and responsibility.

2. Working Hours and Attendance

  1. Working hours shall follow the standards established by Party A, subject to local labor regulations.
  2. Party B shall ensure timely attendance and completion of assigned duties.
  3. Adjustments to working hours may be arranged with mutual agreement.

3. Compensation

  1. Monthly Salary: USD ___________
  2. Salary will be paid monthly to the bank account designated by Party B.
  3. Any bonuses, commissions, or allowances (if applicable) will be based on Party A’s policies.

4. Conduct and Professional Standards

  1. Act professionally and respectfully toward colleagues, clients, and partners.
  2. Follow Party A’s workplace rules, communication guidelines, and promotional standards.
  3. Avoid any behavior that may damage Party A’s reputation, brand image, or customer relations.
  4. Use only approved promotional materials and follow correct representation of the company.

5. Use of Company Resources

  1. If Party A provides equipment, accounts, or promotional materials, Party B shall use them responsibly for work purposes only.
  2. Party B shall not misuse or damage any company property.
  3. Party B shall return all materials upon contract termination.

6. Contract Termination

Party A may terminate the Contract under the following circumstances:

  1. Party B fails to perform job duties or repeatedly neglects responsibilities.
  2. Party B violates company rules, professional standards, or instructions related to promotional activities.
  3. Party B engages in misconduct that negatively affects Party A.
  4. Any other reasons permitted under local labor laws.

Party B may resign by providing written notice ____ days in advance.

7. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this Contract shall first be resolved through friendly negotiation. If negotiation fails, the dispute shall be handled according to the laws and regulations of the location where the Contract is executed.

8. Other Provisions

  1. This Contract becomes effective upon signature by both parties.
  2. Any amendments must be made in written form and agreed by both parties.
  3. Matters not covered in this Contract may be supplemented through additional written agreements.
